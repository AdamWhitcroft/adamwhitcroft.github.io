Tip Tap

Effortless bill calculation

A simple gesture-driven calculator that makes it easy to work out how much to tip, and how to evenly split the bill between friends.

When the bill lands on your table, open Tip Tap and use the keypad to enter the amount.

2

Slide left or right within the Tip cell to set the tip percent.

3

If you're splitting with friends, slide left or right within the Split cell to set how the bill is split.

4

Once you're done, the big number let's you know how much everyone needs to pay. That's it!
